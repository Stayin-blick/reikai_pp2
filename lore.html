The Reikai

In the realm of the Reikai, where the boundaries between mortal and spirit worlds blur...

In the realm of the Reikai, where the boundaries between mortal and spirit worlds blur, vibrant hues painted the skies in shades unseen by mortal eyes. Floating islands adorned with lush foliage drifted lazily amidst wisps of ethereal mist, while cascading waterfalls glistened like liquid crystal, their soothing melodies echoing through the air.

At the heart of the Reikai, a tranquil pool of water surrounded by a narrow strip of land connected to a little island stood a giant tori gate. In a silence that held the breath of eternity, a spark ignited, growing with each passing moment until it filled the gate with a dazzling light.

From its midst emerged a hand, then an arm, and finally, the first Kami in the Reikai stepped forth. Suddenly before the eyes of all who beheld it, hundreds of Kami filled the heart of the Reikai, their presence shimmering with the essence of divine energy.

Days passed peacefully in the Reikai as Kami worked together to build a city to call their own. As the sun dipped below the horizon on the day of the city's completion, the Kami celebrated long into the night, their laughter and revelry echoing across the Reikai. Amidst the bustling energy of the city, Kami feasted, drank, and danced, their spirits soaring with the joy of their achievement. Amongst the cheers and shouts, only a select few discerned whispers carried on the wind, a beckoning from the elder god who had created them, guiding them back to where it all began.

Later that night, as mist enveloped the lake and moonlight bathed the tori gate, the Saru twins, a kindred spirit, and two other Kami answered the call, feeling the weight of destiny upon their shoulders.

Gathered before the gate, a beam of moonlight pierced the mist, illuminating a celestial map onto the floor. The voice of the elder god whispered once more, urging their chosen ones to find what lay hidden. With determination in their hearts, the Kami committed the map to memory, their eyes locking in silent agreement, knowing they had been selected for a greater purpose.

As if they could read each other's minds, they nodded in unison, their resolve unwavering, yet unaware of the dangers lurking ahead. With the whispered guidance of their creator echoing in their ears, they set forth into the unknown, their hearts filled with anticipation and the promise of discovery.
