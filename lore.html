The Reikai

Back 1

In the realm of the Reikai, where the boundaries between mortal and spirit worlds blur, vibrant

hues painted the skies in shades unseen by mortal eyes. Floating islands adorned with lush foliage

drifted lazily amidst wisps of ethereal mist, while cascading waterfalls glistened like liquid

crystal, their soothing melodies echoing through the air.

At the heart of the Reikai, a tranquil pool of water surrounded by a narrow strip of land connected

to a little island stood a giant tori gate. In a silence that held the breath of eternity, a spark

ignited, growing with each passing moment until it filled the gate with a dazzling light. From its

midst emerged a hand, then an arm, and finally, the first Kami in the Reikai stepped forth. Suddenly

before the eyes of all who beheld it, hundreds of Kami filled the heart of the Reikai, their presence

shimmering with the essence of divine energy.
